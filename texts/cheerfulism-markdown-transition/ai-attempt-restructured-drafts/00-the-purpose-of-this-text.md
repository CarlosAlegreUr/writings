# Cheerfulism

*A reflection of existence on itself.*

---

## 0.- The purpose of this text.

Aiming to assure happiness in my life and raise happy children, I might have ended up crafting the final answer to the eternal prosperity of humankind.

How can I be as happy as possible in my life? What will I teach to my future children? Those questions have wandered around my mind since I was 16. Fast forward around five years and I think I found the answer.

I always aimed for general explanations agnostic to me. I was aware of the existence of useful answers according to your specific personality and environment, yet, I was also aware that if any of those ever change, the answers might become useless. Thus I always tried to find the general pattern that could apply to everyone and that would allow me to adapt when facing changes in the future.

The general answers to these questions resulted in this book. Who would have guessed that the answers would make me find the core patterns that allow the best life quality for all humans.

### 0.1.- The structure of the book.

The book has 3 parts and a closing chapter. Each part is divided in different chapters and each chapter in different sections.

**Part 1 is the foundation.** The philosophy that ties all together and from which all is built upon. It explains what I think all of us can do to improve this world forever. It is detailed to the point of explaining basic concepts as it aims to be understood by anyone. This part focuses on the individual, at the person level. If you read nothing else, read this. Cheerfulism is these three chapters.

**Part 2 is an analysis and exploration** on how human societies currently work, the problems they face and how new technologies and the philosophy presented in part 1 can help solve them. This part is slightly more complex as I don't explain all concepts in such a detailed manner as I did in part 1. It is important to say this clearly: Part 2 contains my opinions, my applications of the philosophy. You can disagree with every word in Part 2 and still be a Cheerful. The philosophy lives in Part 1. The opinions live in Part 2.

**Part 3 is grounding.** Where this philosophy came from, where it does not hold, and what I honestly cannot answer. It gives the reader a more solid understanding of the author's possible biases and chains of thought.

Then there is a closing chapter. No new concepts, just me finishing the book.

### 0.2.- How to read this book.

If this is your first read, go sequentially. The concepts build on each other. Every idea in chapter 3 assumes you understood chapters 1 and 2. Every idea in chapter 6 assumes you understood chapters 1 through 5. Skip around and things will feel ungrounded.

If you are coming back to reference something, each concept has one home. It is introduced once, in one place, and after that it is referenced by name. If you forget what the Fixed Variable Resolution Technique is, go to chapter 2. If you forget what "More Enough" means, go to chapter 1. The definitions do not move.

### 0.3.- A disclaimer before we begin.

This book deals in likelihoods, not absolutes.

When I say something works, I mean it increases the probability of a good outcome. When I say something fails, I mean it decreases that probability. I am not saying anything is guaranteed. Biology is statistics. Societies are statistics. Happiness is statistics.

If you read this book looking for absolute truths you will be disappointed. If you read it looking for a way to think about life that consistently improves your odds, I think you will find it useful.

It is all statistics. Keep that in mind through every chapter, every claim, every proposal. Including the ones I'm most confident about.
